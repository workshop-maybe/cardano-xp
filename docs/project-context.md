## What This Is

A feature feedback collection mechanism built on Andamio. NOT learn-to-earn.

**Tagline**: "Support Cardano development, earn rewards."

**Domain**: cardano-xp.io

## What This Proves

1. You can spin up a meaningful Cardano app on top of Andamio in an afternoon
2. XP tokens work as proof-of-contribution without a treasury dependency
3. The "read layer" thesis is real — credentials are composable and self-verifying on-chain
4. Cardano can be used for something other than DeFi yield

## Lesson Coach Pathways (for course creation)

Three entry points for building courses on Cardano XP:

- **Beginner**: "I don't know what the SLTs should be. Help me figure them out."
- **Apprentice**: "I can write SLTs. I need help with lessons."
- **Teacher**: "I know this content. Ready to review and customize."

## Treasury Proposal

Andamio will route a treasury proposal through the platform itself. 100K ADA sponsored. Community meetings to decide what projects to fund and what Andamio features to prioritize. Cardano XP is the first experiment in this model.
